CORE FEATURES

USER PROFILE

Input basic details (age, weight, height, fitness level, goals) that serve as the foundation for highly personalized workout recommendations tailored just for you.

GOAL-BASED AI

Using your profile setup as a basis, our AI generates recommended workouts based on your availability and capability while staying perfectly aligned with your goals.

DAILY ROUTINE

View your recommended exercises in a structured daily routine, complete with exact rep ranges, number of sets, and calculated weights for each specific movement.

EXERCISE LIBRARY

Access a comprehensive list of all exercises available in the app, complete with detailed descriptions, form tips, and high-quality visual demonstrations.

PROGRESS TRACKING

Track your physical journey effectively. Our AI provides a predictive analysis forecasting your specific improvements based on your previous logged workout data.

VIRTUAL TRAINER

Interact with a Virtual Fitness Trainer that acts as your companion, dynamically adjusting your workouts based on your real-time needs and human-level expertise.
